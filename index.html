Homework --

Put as much content as you can into your CVs. Play more with CSS and explore flexbox as a means to layout navigation, contact info, header/main/footer, and article/aside.
Create a print CSS file and include it in the head of your HTML using a link. Think about what you might want to hide or make greyscale when printing your CV. Be sure to visit this article to learn more.

Practice your CSS Selector skills: Flukeout!
